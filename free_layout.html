ABOUT SAMSUNG

A business group with Samsung C&T as its parent company. The group's main businesses are electronics, bio, heavy industries, construction, finance and services. Samsung Group is a governance structure that leads to Chairman Lee Kun-hee → Samsung C&T → Samsung Life Insurance → Samsung Electronics. The company that effectively controls Samsung Group is Samsung C&T, which started as the Samsung Corporation in March 1938, and its flagship unit is Samsung Electronics. Samsung Group is currently the No. 1 business group in Korea in 2017, while Samsung Electronics is the only Korean company to be listed as one of the top 100 companies in the world.
